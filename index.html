Who We Are

Your Trusted Distribution Partner

Festive Food and Beverage Ltd is a leading food and beverage distribution company based in Nicosia, Northern Cyprus. We specialise in supplying a wide range of quality food and beverage products to the hospitality and food service sector.

Our clients include hotels, casinos, restaurants, cafés, and institutional food service providers throughout Northern Cyprus. We are committed to reliable delivery, competitive pricing, and building long-term partnerships with our customers.

With an experienced team and a deep understanding of the local market, Festive Food and Beverage Ltd ensures that every order is handled with care and delivered on time.
